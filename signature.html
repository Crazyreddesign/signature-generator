Step 3

Click on one of the two options to follow further directions for implementing your new html signature.

PC Users

1. Go in to Outlook, and open a new email.

2. Click ‘Signature’ then click ‘Signatures…' within the drop down menu.

3. Under ‘Select signature to edit' click ‘New’ (name it your name) and then paste signature in the empty space. (This is where you can manually delete your cell phone number if you choose not to list one)

4.Make sure to specify to use your new signature for New Messages and/or Replies/Forwards via the drop down shown menu below.

6.Once you have made your edits and have set your default signature press ‘Ok’.

Congratulations you have successfully implemented your new html signature.

Mac Users

1. Go in to Outlook, and open a new email.

2. Click ‘Signature’ then click ‘Signatures…' within the drop down menu.

3. Under ‘Signature name' click ‘+’ (name it your name) and then paste signature in the empty space. (This is where you can manually delete your cell phone number if you choose not to list one)

4.Click on 'Default Signatures…’ under the column Default signature, then click on None and select your new signature from the drop down menu. Press Ok once you finished selecting your default signature.

5.Once you have made your edits and have set your default signature close out of the Signatures window.

Congratulations you have successfully implemented your new html signature.
